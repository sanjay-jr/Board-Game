How to Play 🌈 The Rainbow Garden Adventure 🌈

1. Click "🌟 Start Game" to initialize the board.

2. Click "🎲 Roll Dice" to move your player.

3. The first player to reach the end of the board wins!

4. Use "🔄 Reset" to start a new game.

5. Click the names on the top to rename players before the game starts!

6. Landing on a cell marked as a "shortcut" will advance you further on the board.

7. Landing on a cell marked as a "trap" will send you backwards on the board.

8. Landing on a cell marked as a "orange trap" will send you back to the beginning.

Have fun on your magical journey through the garden!

🎵 "Friends" by Dr. Priscilla Ochran Holt 🎵

“Friends” is an original composition by Priscilla Ochran Holt.

“Friends” was inspired by childhood outdoor activities: hop-scotch, running, and swinging.
